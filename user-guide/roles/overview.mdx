## User Roles Overview

The Intain Markets platform supports multiple user roles, each with specific capabilities, responsibilities, and permissions. Understanding these roles is essential for navigating the platform effectively.

## Role Categories

### 1. Originators & Issuers

**Roles**: Issuer, Market Maker

These users create and manage assets, facilities, and deals on the platform.

### 2. Service Providers

**Roles**: Servicer, Verification Agent, Underwriter, Paying Agent, Rating Agency

These users provide essential services throughout the platform workflows.

### 3. Investors

**Roles**: Lender, Investor

These users provide funding and investment capital.

### 4. Facility Management

**Roles**: Facility Agent

These users manage credit facilities and approve funding.
