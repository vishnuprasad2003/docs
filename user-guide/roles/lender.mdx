## Master Commitment Approval

### Approval Process

**Approval Steps:**
1. **Receive Notification** - Get notified of master commitment
2. **Review Details** - Examine master commitment details
3. **Assess Risk** - Evaluate risk factors
4. **Make Decision** - Approve or decline

**Review Criteria:**
- **Facility Terms** - Interest rates, amounts, terms
- **Collateral Rules** - Collateral eligibility rules
- **Borrower Creditworthiness** - Credit analysis
- **Market Conditions** - Current market conditions

### Approval Actions

#### Approve Master Commitment

**Approval Process:**
1. **Review All Details** - Comprehensive review
2. **Verify Documentation** - Check all documents
3. **Assess Risk** - Evaluate risk factors
4. **Click Approve** - Approve master commitment

**Result:**
- Status changes to **ACTIVE**
- Facility becomes available for funding requests
- Issuer notified of approval

#### Decline Master Commitment

**Decline Process:**
1. **Identify Issues** - Identify decline reasons
2. **Provide Feedback** - Detailed decline feedback
3. **Click Decline** - Decline master commitment
4. **Communicate Decision** - Notify facility agent

**Result:**
- Status remains **PendingLenderApproval**
- Other lenders can still approve
- Facility agent notified of decline
