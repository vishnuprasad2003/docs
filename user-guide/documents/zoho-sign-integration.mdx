## Electronic Signatures Overview

The Intain Markets platform uses **Zoho Sign** for all electronic signature requirements throughout the credit facility workflow. This ensures secure, legally-binding signatures for all critical documents.

---

## When Are Signatures Required?

Electronic signatures are required at key points in the credit facility process:

| Document | Who Signs | When |
|----------|-----------|------|
| **Term Sheet** | Borrower | After creating, before submission |
| **Master Commitment** | Lender | When approving the commitment |
| **Funding Notice** | Facility Agent | For each lender, after tokens created |

---

## How Zoho Sign Works

### The Signing Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive Email Invitation">
    You'll receive an email from Zoho Sign with a link to the document.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Secure Link">
    Open the link to access your document. The link is secure and unique to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Document">
    Read through the entire document carefully before signing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Your Signature">
    Click on the signature fields where indicated to sign electronically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Confirmation">
    After signing, you'll receive a confirmation email with a copy of the signed document.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signature Workflows by Document Type

### Term Sheet Signing

<Accordion title="Term Sheet E-Signature Process" icon="file-contract" defaultOpen>
**Who:** Borrower (Issuer)

**When:** After creating and completing your term sheet, before submission

**Process:**
1. Complete your term sheet in draft status
2. Click "Sign" to initiate Zoho Sign
3. Receive email invitation
4. Review and sign electronically
5. Status changes to "BorrowerSigned"
6. Now you can submit for Facility Agent review
</Accordion>

### Master Commitment Signing

<Accordion title="Master Commitment E-Signature Process" icon="handshake">
**Who:** Lender

**When:** After reviewing master commitment details

**Process:**
1. Facility Agent submits for lender approval
2. Lender receives notification to review
3. Lender reviews master commitment terms
4. Lender signs via Zoho Sign to approve
5. Master Commitment becomes "ACTIVE"
6. Funding requests can now be made
</Accordion>

### Funding Notice Signing

<Accordion title="Funding Notice E-Signature Process" icon="coins">
**Who:** Facility Agent (on behalf of each lender)

**When:** After tokens are created for a funding notice

**Process:**
1. Tokens are created for the funding notice
2. Facility Agent signs for each lender individually
3. Each lender's signature status is tracked separately
4. Once signed, borrower can approve the token transfer
</Accordion>

## Checking Your Email

### What the Email Looks Like

Zoho Sign emails will come from a Zoho Sign address and include:

- **Subject line** mentioning the document type
- **Secure link** to access and sign the document
- **Deadline information** if applicable
- **Contact information** for questions

<Warning>
**Security Tip:** Always verify the email is from Zoho Sign before clicking links. If you're unsure, log into the Intain Markets platform directly to access pending signatures.
</Warning>

### Check Your Spam Folder

If you don't see the Zoho Sign email:

1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Add Zoho Sign to your safe senders list
3. Contact support if you still don't receive it

### Before You Sign

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Review Everything" icon="eye">

    - Verify all details are correct
    - Understand what you're agreeing to

- Follow the on-screen prompts
- Click all required signature fields

- Verify the status updated in the platform
- Proceed with next steps in the workflow

## Security Features

### Document Security

Zoho Sign provides robust security:

| Feature | Protection |
|---------|------------|
| **Encryption** | Documents encrypted during transmission |
| **Access Control** | Only authorized signers can access |
| **Audit Trail** | Complete record of all actions |
| **Tamper Protection** | Signed documents cannot be altered |

### Identity Verification

When you sign, Zoho Sign verifies your identity through:

- **Email authentication** - Link sent only to your email
- **Access tracking** - IP address and browser recorded
- **Signature capture** - Your electronic signature is unique to you

## Legal Validity

Electronic signatures through Zoho Sign are legally binding:

<Note>
Electronic signatures comply with:
- **ESIGN Act** (USA)
- **UETA** (Uniform Electronic Transactions Act)
- **eIDAS** (European Union)
- Most international e-signature standards
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Didn't Receive the Email" icon="envelope">
**What to Do:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Verify your email address in your profile
3. Ask the sender to resend the invitation
4. Contact support if still not received
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Link Doesn't Work" icon="link-slash">
**What to Do:**
1. Try a different browser
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check if link has expired
4. Request a new invitation if needed
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Signature Won't Complete" icon="signature">
**What to Do:**
1. Ensure stable internet connection
2. Try a different browser
3. Make sure all required fields are completed
4. Contact Zoho Sign support for technical issues
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Status Not Updating" icon="arrows-rotate">
**What to Do:**
1. Wait a few minutes for system sync
2. Refresh the platform page
3. Check your confirmation email
4. Contact support if status remains incorrect
</Accordion>
