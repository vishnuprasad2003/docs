## Loan Management Overview

Loan management involves tracking and monitoring individual loans within the credit facility portfolio. This guide covers how to view, track, and manage loan performance.

---

## Portfolio Dashboard

Your portfolio dashboard provides a high-level view of all loans:

### Key Metrics at a Glance

| Metric | What It Shows |
|--------|---------------|
| **Total Loans** | Number of loans in portfolio |
| **Total Value** | Aggregate outstanding balance |
| **Performance Distribution** | Breakdown by performance category |
| **Risk Distribution** | Loans by risk level |

## Individual Loan Details

### Viewing Loan Information

Each loan record displays:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Basic Info">
    - **Loan ID** - Unique identifier
    - **Borrower Name** - Who the loan is to
    - **Original Amount** - Initial loan amount
    - **Current Balance** - Outstanding amount
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Terms">
    - **Interest Rate** - Current rate
    - **Maturity Date** - When loan matures
    - **Payment Amount** - Regular payment
    - **Payment Frequency** - Monthly, etc.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Performance">
    - **Current Status** - Performing, delinquent, etc.
    - **Days Delinquent** - If applicable
    - **Payment History** - Recent payment activity
    - **Risk Rating** - Current risk assessment
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Collateral">
    - **Property Type** - Residential, commercial, etc.
    - **Property Value** - Current estimated value
    - **LTV Ratio** - Loan-to-value percentage
    - **Location** - Property location
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Risk Assessment

### Risk Levels

Loans are categorized by risk:

| Risk Level | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Low** | Strong payment history, stable financials |
| **Medium** | Some concerns, needs monitoring |
| **High** | Significant risk factors present |
| **Critical** | Immediate attention required |

### Risk Factors

The platform considers these factors when assessing risk:

- **Payment History** - Past payment performance
- **Financial Condition** - Borrower's financial health
- **Market Conditions** - Economic and market factors
- **Collateral Value** - Current property valuation

## Working with Problem Loans

### Early Intervention

For loans showing early warning signs:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify Issues Early">
    Use monitoring tools to catch problems before they escalate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Borrower">
    Reach out to understand the situation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Develop Solutions">
    Work with borrower on payment arrangements if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Document Everything">
    Keep detailed records of all communications and actions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Delinquency Management

For loans that become delinquent:

| Days Delinquent | Typical Actions |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| **1-30 days** | Payment reminders, phone contact |
| **31-60 days** | Formal demand letters, payment plans |
| **61-90 days** | Escalated collection efforts |
| **90+ days** | Legal review, workout options |
