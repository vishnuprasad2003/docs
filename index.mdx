## Welcome to Intain Markets

**Intain Markets** is a comprehensive blockchain-based digital platform that transforms asset-backed finance through secure, transparent, and efficient workflows. Our platform enables financial institutions, servicers, and investors to manage credit facilities, securitization deals, whole loan investments, and more with complete transparency and regulatory compliance.

## What is Intain Markets?

Intain Markets is a next-generation financial technology platform that combines the power of blockchain, IPFS storage, and smart contract automation to digitize and streamline asset-backed financial operations. The platform serves multiple market segments through dedicated workflows, all built on a unified infrastructure.

## Platform Capabilities

### Multi-Workflow Support

The Intain Markets platform supports multiple financial workflows, each with its own specialized features:

- **Credit Facility Flow**: Term sheet → Master commitment → Funding request → Funding notice
- **Whole Loan Flow**: Loan origination → Loan servicing → Payment processing → Reporting  
- **Securitization Flow**: Pool creation → Deal onboarding → Tranche creation → Investment management
- **Preview Flow**: Pool creation → Loan tape upload → Verification → NFT minting
